
Point Your Destination!
=======
本模组需要安装Distant Horizon与Xaero‘s minimap作为前置，由于作者的能力与经验问题，暂时无法判断这两项前置的版本需求范围，因此取消了前置的版本限制。

本模组的灵感显然来源于《塞尔达传说：旷野之息》中的望远镜标点功能，作者很喜欢这种从远距离标记目标点然后朝着它前进的感觉。

本模组允许你在手持望远镜右键观察时，按下左键并在视线位置放下一个Xaero‘s小地图的航路点

同时，它也支持你使用这种方法在未加载的远距离区块通过遥远地平线的API添加航路点

注意：受到DH raycast的计算瓶颈，如果你的模组环境对设备压力大，可能需要较长时间（少则几秒多则十多秒）才能标记一个1000m以上的点位

This mod requires Distant Horizons and Xaero's Minimap as dependencies. Due to the author's limited ability and experience, the required version range for these two dependencies cannot be determined at this time, so the version restrictions for them have been removed.

This mod is clearly inspired by the spyglass pinning feature in The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild, as the author really enjoys the feeling of marking a distant target and heading towards it.

This mod allows you to place a Xaero's Minimap waypoint at your line of sight by pressing the left mouse button while holding and looking through a spyglass with the right mouse button.

Meanwhile, it also allows you to use this method to add waypoints in unloaded distant chunks through Distant Horizons' API.

Note: Due to the calculation bottleneck of DH raycasting, if your modpack places high stress on your device, 

it may take a significant amount of time (ranging from a few seconds to over ten seconds)to mark a waypoint that is more than 1000m away.
